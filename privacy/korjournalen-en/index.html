Privacy Policy for Mileage Log

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MPK Tec AB ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Mileage Log app (the "App"). Our core principle is to respect your privacy. We have designed the App so that your data stays on your device and in your own iCloud account.

Data We Store

Mileage Log stores only the data you create in the App.

  • Trip data: Trip dates, times, distances, start/end locations, purpose, comments, project details, and route points.
  • Vehicle, driver, and company data: Information you add for reporting and organization.
  • Odometer readings: Annual readings you enter manually.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled, your data is synchronized through Apple CloudKit between your devices using your own iCloud account. We do not operate any servers that store your trip data.

Location & Motion Data

  • Location (GPS): Used to record trips when you start a GPS trip. Location data is stored only on your device and in your iCloud.
  • Motion & Fitness: Used to provide smart reminders for manually started trips. Motion data is processed locally on your device.

We do not access your location or motion data unless you start a GPS trip or enable features that require it.

CarPlay

The App uses Apple's official CarPlay integration to support trip start/ stop. CarPlay usage does not send data to us or third parties.

Reports & Export

Reports (PDF/CSV) are generated locally on your device. If you share a report, it is shared by you through the standard iOS share sheet.

No Tracking or Third-Party Analytics

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Payments

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. We only receive an anonymous confirmation from Apple that a purchase was made.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
