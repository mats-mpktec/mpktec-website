About us

MPK Tec AB is a Swedish technology company dedicated to developing user-friendly and efficient software solutions. We aim to simplify digital processes for both individuals and businesses.

Our philosophy is based on innovation, security, and a strong customer focus. We are proud to create products that make a real difference in our users' lives.

We prioritize solutions that run locally on your device when there is no clear benefit in using cloud services. This ensures high reliability, lowers costs, and protects your privacy.
